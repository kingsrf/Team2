Safety Tips & Best Times to Visit Rampart Ridge

Safety Tips

  • Wear sturdy hiking boots to navigate uneven terrain.
  • Carry a map, compass, or GPS device to avoid getting lost.
  • Pack enough water and snacks to stay hydrated and energized.
  • Check weather conditions before starting your hike.
  • Stay on marked trails to protect both yourself and the environment.

Best Times to Visit

Rampart Ridge is best visited during summer (June to August) and early fall (September to October). During these seasons, the weather is mild, and the trails are generally clear. Avoid visiting in winter as snow and ice can make the trails hazardous.
